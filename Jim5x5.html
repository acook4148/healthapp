Jim Wendlers 5/3/1 Program

This program is based on a 4 week rotation with 4 workouts a week. It is designed to be modifiable according to your goals and preferences.

General Rep Scheme

Week Reps
Week 1 3x5
Week 2 3x3
Week 3 5, 3, 1+
Week 4 3x5 (Deload)

The goal is to build up to high intensity PR's on week 3 while still managing fatigue and volume

The primary exercises are for you to choose for yourself. If you are dealing with a certain injury you can modify the exercises accordingly.

This is Jim Wendler's original plan and has a good balance between upper and lower body exercises. It is however recommended to use compound movements for these main lifts.

Assistance exercises are left entirely up to you. As an intermediate lifter you should know your favorite accessories and the rep ranges to do them in, as long as you are recovering between workouts.
